Custom render objects let you bypass the widget layer entirely to implement effects and layouts that aren't otherwise possible. These examples cover the most common reasons developers reach for a custom render object, with enough detail to adapt each pattern to your own use case.

## In this section

- [Custom Scrolling](/examples/custom-scrolling) — Scroll physics and viewport layouts beyond what Flutter's built-in slivers support.
- [Particle Effects](/examples/particle-effects) — Thousands of animated particles per frame without widget tree overhead.
- [Data Visualization](/examples/data-visualization) — 60fps charts, graphs, and heatmaps with full gesture and hit-test support.
- [Game UI](/examples/game-ui) — Custom clip paths, layered paint operations, and frame-perfect animations.
